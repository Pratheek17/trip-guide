Europe's largest medieval square (since 1257). Coffee, let Nived chase pigeons. Trumpet call (hejnal) from St Mary's tower every hour — cuts off mid-note to commemorate a 13th century watchman shot by Mongol arrows.
Walking Route

⛏️Wieliczka Salt Mine
UNESCO since 13th century. 2.5km underground, 800 steps DOWN, lift up. 14°C — bring fleece. Chapel of St. Kinga: entire cathedral carved from salt including chandeliers. Audio guide for Nived. 2.5-3 hours.
BOOKED 9AM. CASH for lower-level gift shop. Salt lamps with amethyst PLN 100-300.
History: Salt was worth its weight in gold. This mine funded Polish kings for centuries. Operated 1248-2007.
